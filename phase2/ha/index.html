Class: cohere.owl:connection

Label: Connection

Description: A connection of two abstract_ideas through a relation. Since a connection is itself an abstract_ideas, the connection can also link two connections, or one connection with a node (= a simple idea) . in other words, a connection can be used as a node in a new connection. At the moment, we only provide support for representing binary connections only (two nodes only). Moreover, when a connection is instantiated, it is also possible to define the node_types of the two ideas which are connected. The node_types are also called the 'roles' or 'contexts' of the ideas.
